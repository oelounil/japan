COVID-19 Health & Safety

Masks are strongly recommended in Japan and some places may refuse entry if you are not wearing a mask. Please follow physical distancing guidelines of staying 2m away from people when you are interacting, to help us prevent the spread of COVID-19. Please practice good hygiene by washing your hands thoroughly.

Transport in Japan

Japan has an extensive public transport system and you can go just about anywhere with it. You can use a prepaid Welcome Suica card to to automatically calculate and deduct fares for travel when you tap the card at ticket gates. It can also be used at some stores for shopping.

Wi-Fi

In major metropolitan areas, there may be free Wi-Fi visitors can connect to. However for guaranteed access to the internet, consider renting a personal hotspot. Rental kiosks are available at most airports. Another option is to purchase a travel sim card on a prepaid plan.

Language

Japanese is the main language spoken, however major cities and tourist areas will generally understand English. Feel free to use a translator application during your journey.
